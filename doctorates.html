Honorary Doctorates

From Middle English doctor, doctour "an expert, authority on a subject" from Anglo-Norman doctour from Latin doctor (“teacher”) from doceō (“I teach”). Displaced native Middle English lerare "doctor, teacher" (from Middle English leren "to teach, instruct" from Old English lǣran, lēran "to teach, instruct, guide", compare Old English lārēow "teacher, master"), or a person who has special knowledge or talents.

 

Many have served in various Ministry positions and became rich with lots of knowledge and experience in their fields, but do not have any formal certificate.

 

We would like to honor those who had gained real life Ministry experience by offering an Honorary Degree showing that they earned enough experience to become experts in their fields. We welcome the opportunity to receive Recommendations and Nominations of persons in the ministry who deserve the Special Recognition and Honor of receiving an Honorary Degree.

 

The applicant must meet the criteria established by the Power Institute Of leadership and the Board of Power Ministries International Inc., The Honorary Doctor of Divinity Degree (D.D) will be conferred upon Ministers, Christian Leaders or Public Servants who have made significant contributions in their respective fields or for the extension of God's Kingdom in one of our Conferences. You can contact us for the details of the next conference.

 

Power Institute does not sell its Honorary Degrees, however there is a suggested donation amount. All Honorary Degrees are awarded solely for the accomplishments and contributions that a person has made.

I. Ministerial License

A ministerial license will be granted with the following conditions:

  1. The candidate can show documentation of an ongoing involvement in a Christian ministry.
  2. This involvement must have been ongoing for a period of at least three years at the time of application.
  3. The candidate must provide 3 letters of recommendation. One of these letters should be from the pastor or overseer (Note: If you are a pastor, this letter should be from a person to whom you hold yourself accountable.)
  4. The candidate must successfully complete the Diploma course from Power Institute Of Leadership and attend to at least one Power Leadership Training Conference.
  5. If the candidate can show reason, why one or more of these conditions may be waived, we will take that reason into consideration for reassessment and reevaluation.

Doctoral Dissertation Guidelines

You must follow these Guidelines without fail. Any deviation from the Guidelines will result in losing the grades.

  1. Submit a proposal by postal mail to Power Institute of Leadership.
  2. The proposal must contain:
    • A title
    • A Thesis Statement, a carefully made statement expressing what is going to be proven in the Thesis.
    • An outline of your Dissertation.
  3. As soon as you have received the approval of your Proposal, you can start working on the dissertation.
  4. The thesis must be submitted in duplicate. One copy will remain with Power Institute of Leadership.
  5. It must be bound. Your name should be clearly mentioned on the cover.
  6. The finished dissertation must contain your previously approved thesis statement. You are not allowed to change the originally approved Thesis Statement.
  7. Leave an approval page in the front. It should simply be headed "e;Approval Page"e;.
  8. Leave the backside of each page blank.
  9. Always start chapters a new page with the heading spaced down one/third of the way from the top of the page.
  10. The Dissertation must contain a minimum of 40,000 words - About 200 pages.
  11. The typing must be double spaced.
  12. It must reflect at least 50 cited works.
  13. It should contain:
    • A Table of Contents
    • An Introduction
    • Footnotes or endnotes
    • A Bibliography

Guidelines for the Masters’ Dissertation

You must follow these Guidelines without fail. Any deviation from the Guidelines will result in losing the grades.

  1. Submit a proposal by postal mail to Power Institute of Leadership.
  2. The thesis must contain a thesis statement. A thesis statement is one sentence that expresses what your thesis shall prove. This statement is the theme of the thesis.
  3. Your work must be submitted in duplicate.
  4. It must be bound. Your name should be clearly indicated on the cover.
  5. Write a minimum of 12,000 words - About 50 pages
  6. Reflect at least 10 cited works.
  7. It will include:
    • Table of Contents
    • Introduction
    • Bibliography
    • End or foot notes
