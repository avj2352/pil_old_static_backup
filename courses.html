Courses Offered

The following are the list of courses offered online:

 
  1. Diploma in Christian Leadership - 6 months
  2. Diploma in Christian Management - 6 Months
  3. B.Th - 3 years – 120 Credits
  4. M.Th - 2 years - 90 Credits
  5. M.Div - 2 years - 90 Credits
  6. M.B.A - 2 Years - Christian Management – 90 Credits
  7. M.B.A – 2 Years - Christian Leadership – 90 Credits
  8. Doctor of Ministry (D.Min) - 3 Years - 60 Credits +
    • 1 Research Course + Dissertation
  9.  Honorary Doctorate- (D.D)
 

Diploma:

This is part of the effort to train the Pastor and leader so that they will be more fruitful in the Ministry. This will benefit those who are active in the churches and mission organizations.  

B.Th:

This is for those who are keen to study the Bible, its interpretation and looking forward for developing an analytical and evaluative skill which will enhance your Ministry altogether. A minimum of High School Education with proficiency in English Language or entire High School Education in English Medium. Experience in the Ministry for a minimum of 5 years or some Bible College Training.  

M.Div:

This program enables the leader to grasp God’s Mission and how can it be accomplished through Effective Evangelism and Disciple Making. This course also will help the leader to interpret the scriptures and Biblical truths effectively. Not only will the theoretical aspects, this course help to be successful in the Ministry. Must have an earned B.Th or its academic equivalence.  

M.B.A:

Along with the Vision, every leader should be able to ‘manage’ also to reach the goal. This course will help the leader to use the resources in an effective manner to reach the desired level.  

Doctor Of Ministry ( D.Min )

This is for the leaders who are actively engaged in the Ministry. This will enhance the professional competence in the specific area and calling of the leader. This maintains the highest academic standards and helping the leader to reach his maximum potential in his calling with a visible sign of personal and spiritual growth. This program is having 60 credits and Dissertation.  

Requirements:

Have an earned M.Th or M.Div or its academic equivalents.
Must be currently active in the Ministry
Have at least two years of Ministry experience.

 

Tests:

Quarterly tests will be given in the form of Multiple Choice or short essay type questions. Grades will be given as A, B, C, D and F.

 

Grading:
1. 100-94 A
2. 93-87 B
3. 86-79 C
4. 78-71 D
5. 70 and below E
6. President's Honor Roll 4.00
7. Dean's List 3.50 - 3.99
8. Honor Roll 3.20 - 3.49
9. Summa Cum Laude 8% - 100%
10. Magna Cum Laude 4% - 97%
11. Cum Laude 90% - 93%
